About Veronica

I have been practicing yoga for over 15 years, but really started to notice the benefits of practicing consistently when life got a bit challenging — yoga for me has been such a great tool for becoming more centred and feeling well in myself. It has also allowed me to become stronger and keep working on my balance, especially important as I get that bit older. With a diagnosis of auto-immune arthritis, I understand that a yoga practice can feel different from day to day, and that we can respond to what our bodies need through challenge, modification, or rest. Having recently completed my 200 hour teacher training with Paula and Pol at Yoga Meditation Healing in Glasgow, I'm eager to continue learning and enjoy practicing with others like you! If you have any questions do get in touch: info@yogawithveronica.co.uk

Types of Classes

Vinyasa Flow (all levels)

Thursdays · 9:30–10:30 am

Morningside Parish Church

Book

We begin with a grounding practice to connect the mind with body and breath.

You will have the opportunity to move, open, and build strength, as well as develop mind-body awareness. The class will enable you to explore what your body needs, with guidance for adaptations and variations provided. You will leave feeling grounded, calm, and energised for the day.

*Please bring your own mat and any props such as blocks.
*This class is not suitable during pregnancy.

Beginners Yoga

Fridays · 9:30–10:30 am

Morningside Parish Church

Book

This class is for beginners, or those returning to a weekly yoga class following a break.

The postures will include poses on the floor, standing up and sitting down, allowing your body to develop and maintain flexibility, mobility and strength. Simple breathing practices (pranayama), meditation, and guided relaxation will be part of the class.

The class will give you confidence to begin your own yoga practice at home if you wish, and is designed to help you release any stress that may have built up through the week. You will leave feeling refreshed and ready for the day.

Please bring a yoga mat and two yoga blocks. If you have any questions please get in touch.

*This class is not suitable during pregnancy.
